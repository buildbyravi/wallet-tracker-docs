Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

Wallet Balance Tracker is a free Google Sheets™ add-on developed by antigravitylabs. It helps you track cryptocurrency wallet balances across EVM-compatible blockchains. This policy explains what data exists, where it lives, and what we do (and don't do) with it.

What we collect

Information you provide

  • Wallet addresses — public addresses you enter into your Sheet
  • RPC endpoint URLs — blockchain node URLs you configure

Information we do NOT collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Private keys or seed phrases — we never ask for these
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints

How your data is used

The add-on operates entirely inside your Google Sheets™ environment:

  • Wallet addresses are sent only to the RPC endpoints you configure, to query public blockchain data
  • Balance results are written back to your Sheet and nowhere else
  • We operate no backend servers that receive or store your wallet data

Data storage

All data lives in your Google Sheet. We have no database. Google's own Privacy Policy governs how Google handles your Sheets data.

Third-party services

RPC providers

When you fetch balances, wallet addresses are sent to the blockchain node URL you configured (e.g. Infura, Alchemy, a public RPC). Each provider has its own privacy policy. Use reputable providers and review their terms before use.

Google Services

The add-on uses Google Workspace APIs. We request the minimum permissions required — read/write access to your current spreadsheet only.

Security

  • All network communication uses HTTPS
  • The add-on runs in your browser — no data passes through our servers
  • We cannot access your Sheets without your explicit permission grant
⚠️ Never enter private keys or seed phrases into your Sheet or anywhere in the add-on. The add-on only needs public wallet addresses.

Your rights

  • Access — your data is in your Sheet, always visible to you
  • Edit/delete — modify or remove any data in your Sheet at any time
  • Uninstall — removing the add-on or deleting the Sheet removes all associated data
  • Export — use Google Sheets' standard export features

Children's privacy

The add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Open source

The add-on code is publicly available. You can audit exactly what it does at: github.com/antigravitylabs/wallet-tracker-docs
