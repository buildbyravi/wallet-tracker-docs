2. What the Add-on does

The Add-on is a free tool that tracks cryptocurrency wallet balances across EVM-compatible blockchains inside Google Sheets™. Specifically, it:

  • Queries public blockchain data via RPC endpoints you configure
  • Writes balance information to your Google Sheet
  • Operates entirely within your Google Sheets™ environment
  • Does not collect or store your data on any server we control
