# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: June 8, 2026**

## Overview

Wallet Balance Tracker ("the Add-on") is a free Google Sheets add-on developed by antigravitylabs that helps users track cryptocurrency wallet balances across multiple EVM-compatible blockchains.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Wallet Addresses**: You manually enter cryptocurrency wallet addresses into your Google Sheet
- **RPC URLs**: You configure RPC endpoints to query blockchain data
- **Spreadsheet Data**: Any data you enter into your Google Sheets

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- **Personal Information**: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
- **Private Keys**: We never request or access your private keys or seed phrases
- **Email Addresses**: We do not collect your email address or contact information
- **Usage Analytics**: We do not track how you use the add-on
- **Cookies**: We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

## How We Use Your Information

The Add-on operates entirely within your Google Sheets environment:
- Wallet addresses are used ONLY to query public blockchain data via RPC endpoints
- All queries are made directly from your browser to the RPC endpoints you configure
- We do not store, log, or transmit your wallet addresses to any server we control
- Balance data is written directly to your Google Sheet and is not sent anywhere else

## Data Storage

All data remains in your Google Sheet:
- Your wallet addresses, RPC configurations, and balance data stay in your own Google Sheet
- Google's privacy policy and terms of service govern how Google handles your Sheet data
- We do not maintain any databases or servers that store your information

## Third-Party Services

### RPC Providers
The Add-on makes requests to blockchain RPC endpoints that you configure:
- These are public blockchain nodes (e.g., Infura, Alchemy, or public RPCs)
- Your wallet addresses are sent to these services to query balance information
- Each RPC provider has its own privacy policy
- We recommend using reputable RPC providers and reviewing their privacy practices

### Google Services
The Add-on uses Google Workspace APIs:
- Subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- We request minimal permissions (read/write to your current spreadsheet only)

## Data Security

- All communications use HTTPS encryption
- The Add-on runs in your browser and does not send data to our servers
- We cannot access your spreadsheets without your explicit permission
- You control all data by managing your own Google Sheet

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: Your data is always in your Google Sheet
- **Modify**: Edit or delete any data in your Sheet at any time
- **Delete**: Uninstall the Add-on or delete your Sheet to remove all data
- **Export**: Use Google Sheets' export features to download your data

## Children's Privacy

The Add-on is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
