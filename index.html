How it works

  1. Install the add-on

    Find "Wallet Balance Tracker" in the Google Workspace™ Marketplace and install.

  2. Configure your RPC endpoint

    Paste in any public or private RPC URL — Infura, Alchemy, QuickNode, or your own node.

  3. Enter wallet addresses

    Add public wallet addresses (0x...) to your Sheet. Private keys are never needed.

  4. Fetch balances

    Click Refresh — balances appear in your Sheet, ready to use in formulas or charts.
